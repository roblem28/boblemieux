Earth Twin is a browser-based 3D digital twin of Earth built entirely on free public data — NASA Blue Marble imagery, VIIRS night lights, ESRI satellite tiles, AWS terrain elevation, and OpenFreeMap 3D buildings. Renders seamlessly from orbital altitude to street level with 3D terrain exaggeration, animated clouds, atmospheric glow, and real city geometry. Day/night mode, auto-spin, city search, quick descent to 10 global cities, full keyboard control. Built with MapLibre GL JS, zero API keys.
